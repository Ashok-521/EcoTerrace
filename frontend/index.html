About EcoTerrace

Making Urban Gardening Simple

EcoTerrace was born from a simple idea: everyone should be able to grow their own food, regardless of space limitations. We combine technology with agricultural science to help urban dwellers create thriving terrace gardens.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable urban living by making terrace gardening accessible, enjoyable, and successful for everyone.
